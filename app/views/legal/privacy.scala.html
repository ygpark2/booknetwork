Privacy Policy

BookNetwork stores account details needed for authentication and book lending, including your name, email, and the activity you create in the app.

We use this data to operate book listings, comments, bookmarks, follows, loans, and moderation reports. We do not expose password hashes in the UI.

If you need data removal or account cleanup, an administrator must handle that request because self-service deletion is not implemented yet.
